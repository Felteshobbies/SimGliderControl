# SimGliderControl - Print Instructions

## Step 5: 3D Printing Handles and Control Elements

###  Flap/Trim Handles (Flexible Mount)
**Material:** PETG (black)  
**Layer Height:** 0.2 mm  
**Perimeters:** 3  
**Infill Density:** 30%  
**Support:** no

**Parts to print:**
- 2× `GliderCtrlHandles_v1-TrimFlap` *(for adjustable angle mounting)*

or use `GliderCtrlHandles_v1-TrimFlap60` *(60° fixed angle)*  or  `GliderCtrlHandles_v1-TrimFlap90` *(90° fixed angle)*


### Trim Knob
**Material:** PLA (green)  
**Layer Height:** 0.2 mm  
**Perimeters:** 3  
**Infill Density:** 30%  
**Support:** Yes - support on build plate only

**Parts to print:**
- 1× `GliderCtrlHandles_v1-TrimKnob`

**Print Notes:** 
- Green color matches authentic glider trim knobs

###  Winch Release Components
**Material:** PETG (yellow)  
**Layer Height:** 0.2 mm  
**Perimeters:** 3  
**Infill Density:** 100%  
**Support:** Yes - support on build plate only

**Parts to print:**
- 1× `GliderCtrlHandles_v1-WinchRelease`
- 1× `GliderCtrlHandles_v1-WinchReleaseMount`

---

###  Position Pointer
**Material:** PETG (white or on your choice)  
**Layer Height:** 0.2 mm  
**Perimeters:** 3  
**Infill Density:** 100%  
**Support:** None

**Parts to print:**
- 1× `GliderCtrCarriage_v1-Pointer`

**Print Notes:** 
- White provides high contrast against black panel for easy position reading
- 100% infill ensures rigidity
