# SimGliderControl - Print Instructions

## Step 1: 3D Printing the Base Components

### Print Settings Overview
All parts should be printed in the same orientation as shown in the STL,3mf files.

---

### Group 1: Guide Shafts and Magnet Holders
**Material:** PLA  
**Layer Height:** 0.2 mm  
**Perimeters:** 2  
**Infill Density:** 15%  
**Support:** None

**Parts to print:**
- 3× `GliderCtrBase_v1-GuideShaft`
- 3× `GliderCtrBase_v1-Magnetholder`

**Print Notes:** These lightweight parts don't require high strength, so minimal infill is sufficient.

---

### Group 2: Linear Guides
**Material:** PLA  
**Layer Height:** 0.2 mm  
**Perimeters:** 5  
**Infill Density:** 100%  
**Support:** None

**Parts to print:**
- 3× `GliderCtrBase_v1-LinearGuide`

**Print Notes:** These parts require high strength and dimensional accuracy for smooth slider movement. The increased perimeters and 100% infill ensure durability and smoothness.

---

### Group 3: Mount Assemblies
**Material:** PLA (PETG recommended for better durability)  
**Layer Height:** 0.2 mm  
**Perimeters:** 4  
**Infill Density:** 40%  
**Support:** Yes - support on build plate only

**Parts to print:**
- 1× `GliderCtrBase_v1-Mount1Assembly`
- 1× `GliderCtrBase_v1-Mount2Assembly`

**Print Notes:** These mounting brackets will bear mechanical loads. PETG is recommended for better layer adhesion and strength.
